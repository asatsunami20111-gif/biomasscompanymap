## Ethos - Many of the companies have the biodegradability and long-term use of their materials at the forefront.

## Companies who share this ethos
Companies prioritising compostability, bio-based polymers, or reprocessing:

- [[Spinnova]] – Fully recyclable cellulose, biodegradable.
    
- [[Infinited]] – Cotton waste → new fibre, repeatably recyclable.
    
- [[My-Fi]]– Mycelium = home compostable and biodegradable.
    
- [[QMILK]]– Biodegradable casein fibres.
    
- [[ScobyTec]] – 100% bacterial cellulose that biodegrades rapidly.
    
- [[Bio-LUSH]],[[Tera Mira]], [[ALLGAE]], [[KelpTex]] – Seaweed fibres biodegradable.
    
- [[Knokkon]], [[Nettlecraft]], [[LCN]] – Natural bast fibres.
    
- [[BioPuff]] – Cellulose-based, compostable insulation.
    
- [[Ananas Anam]] / [[BANANATEX]] – Natural fibres (varying depending on PU content).
