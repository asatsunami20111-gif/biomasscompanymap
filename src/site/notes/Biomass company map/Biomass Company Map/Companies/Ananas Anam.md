Pinatex 


Mission:
Using Pineapple waste products, Ananas Anam consider the social impact for pineapple farmers ([[Supporting Local and Regional Supply Chains]]) and Waste valorisation of food production ([[Valorisation and Repurposing of Waste Streams]]).

## Sustainability Goals:
They aim to Net-zero or Lower Fossil Fuel Dependance]] , have Reduced Water and Energy Footprints]] and Lower Chemical Usage by using agricultural waste instead of growing new crops. 

By supporting rural economies they also have Socio-Economic Considerations]]. 

## Material Goals
They strive for Process Control for Chemical use and Pollution]] and Performance Superiority or Parity]] by improving durability while avoiding petroleum based coatings.

## Justifications:
They build supply chains with Filipino pineapple farmers and provide [[Economic Transformation for Producers]] and [[Circular Economy and Resource Efficiency]]. They also uphold the values of a [[Transparency and Consumer Focused. 

They mechanically extract their fibres and finish without toxic solvents, achieving [[Pollution and Chemical Reduction]].
